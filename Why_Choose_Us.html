What Makes Sunset Garden
Truly Special

01 — Our Team

Compassionate,
Qualified Staff

Every member of our team is not only qualified and experienced, but genuinely motivated by care. We hire for character as much as credentials — and we invest continuously in training, supervision, and staff wellbeing, because happy, supported carers give the best care.

02 — Individual Plans

Personalised
Care Plans

No two residents are alike. Each person who comes to Sunset Garden receives a fully individualised care plan developed in collaboration with them and their family — reviewed regularly and adapted as needs evolve over time.

03 — Family Partnership

Open Family
Communication

We treat families as true partners in care. Regular updates, a dedicated family liaison, open visiting hours, and transparent reporting mean you are always informed, always included, and always at ease.
