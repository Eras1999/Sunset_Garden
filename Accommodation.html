Room Options at
Sunset Garden

Every room is a thoughtful space — furnished with warmth, equipped with care, and designed to feel genuinely like home.

Private Room

A fully private, elegantly furnished room with en-suite or adjacent bathroom, wardrobe, writing desk, and garden or courtyard views. Complete personal space and privacy.

  • En-suite or private bathroom
  • Wardrobe & personal storage
  • Emergency call system
  • Personalisation welcome

Shared Room

A comfortable shared room for residents who enjoy companionship — with private sleeping zones, shared sitting area, and designed for friendship and gentle social connection.

  • Private curtained sleeping zones
  • Shared bathroom facilities
  • Built-in companionship
  • Emergency call system

Garden View Room

Our most sought-after accommodation — private rooms with large windows or a private veranda overlooking the tropical garden. Morning light and birdsong included.

  • Garden-facing windows/veranda
  • Premium furnishings
  • Direct garden access
  • All private room features
