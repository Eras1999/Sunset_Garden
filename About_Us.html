Our Story

Rooted in Love,
Built over Decades

Sunset Garden was born from a personal conviction: that elders deserve not merely to be looked after, but to be celebrated. Founded in 2009 by Dr. Amali Wickramasinghe, herself a geriatrician, the residence began as a modest home in Colombo and grew, through word and trust, into the premier elder care destination it is today.

What began with six residents and a small team has grown into a community of over 120 residents supported by 45 dedicated professionals — yet our founding philosophy has never changed. Every face we see every morning is someone's mother, father, grandmother, grandfather. We treat them accordingly.

Over the years we have earned the trust of hundreds of families across Sri Lanka, and the warmth of those relationships is the truest measure of everything we do.

What Drives Us

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide compassionate, person-centred care in a safe, joyful home environment — honouring the individuality, dignity, and wellbeing of every resident, and offering their families complete peace of mind.

Our Vision

To be Sri Lanka's most trusted elder care community — a place where ageing is experienced with grace, purpose, and deep belonging, and where families know their loved ones are thriving.

How We Care

A Philosophy of
Quiet Compassion

We believe care is not a service to be delivered; it is a relationship to be nurtured. Our approach is guided by four values that shape every interaction, every decision, and every moment at Sunset Garden.

Dignity Above All

Every resident is treated as a whole person, with full respect for their history, choices, and personality.

Excellence Without Exception

We hold ourselves to the highest standards in care, nutrition, safety, and environment.

Community & Connection

We nurture genuine bonds between residents, families, and staff — because belonging heals.

Joy in the Everyday

Small moments — a shared meal, a sunny walk, a familiar song — are the fabric of a meaningful life.
