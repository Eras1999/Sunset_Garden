Our Story

A Home Built on
Love & Dignity

Sunset Garden was founded with a single vision: to create a place where elders are not merely cared for, but genuinely cherished. Nestled in serene surroundings, our residence blends the warmth of a family home with the excellence of professional care.

Every member of our family — residents, staff, and visitors — shares in a community built on compassion, respect, and the simple joy of life's quiet, beautiful moments.
